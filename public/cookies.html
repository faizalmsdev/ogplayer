How to Get YouTube Cookies

  1. Install a cookie extraction browser extension:
    • Chrome: "Get cookies.txt LOCALLY" or "cookies.txt"
    • Firefox: "cookies.txt" add-on
  2. Go to YouTube.com and log in to your account
  3. Use the extension to export cookies for youtube.com
  4. Copy the cookies content and paste it below
  5. Click "Upload Cookies" to save them to the server

Note: Cookies help bypass YouTube's anti-bot measures on servers.
